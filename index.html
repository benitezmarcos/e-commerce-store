Summer Days in Melilla

Light Jackets, ideal for warm evenings.

The latest Club Coral collection takes inspiration from Agostinho de Olivera’s travels through the southern Mediterranean in the 1970s and 80s. His early visits to Spanish cities in Morocco such as Ceuta and Melilla in particular played an important role in shaping his taste in color, textures, and fabrics. Agostinho claims that in this period of his life he experienced how reliability and practicality can go hand in hand at no cost of style.
